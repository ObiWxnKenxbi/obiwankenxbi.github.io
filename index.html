About me

Hello there, general kenobi

My name is Sofia, I'm an audio engineer from Mexico City, Mexico, currently living in Barcelona, Spain. I got my bachelor's degree from REC Música, Centro de Estudios Musicales in December 2020 with a GPA of 9.21 and currently I`m studying a masters degree in Sound and Music Computing at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain. I completed my internships at Woodlock Studios and at Topetitud Studios in Mexico City in 2020. During this time, I focused on the equipment repair, the studio's signal flow, and I also helped with the recording sessions. Furthermore, I've had the chance to work with different companies and artists, helping them with their social media marketing, and developing and designing their web pages. For the past two years, I have focused more on Artificial Intelligence and DSP for audio. I have taken some courses related to programming and audio coding and I have been working on my own plugins, hoping that I will be able to specialize in audio coding and DSP in the future. I also love digital illustration and I do some in my free time.
